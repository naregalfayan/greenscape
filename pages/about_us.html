About Us

At GreenScape our mission is to bring back the culture of planting green life. The greener environment has many advantages,
which includes saving planet by making it greener, cleaner and healthier. The problem is that nowadays there are a lot of trees
being cut down and plants are being destroyed by man or by wildfires. Trees are used for making paper, for making furniture
and for many other purposes. Eventually humanity will face serious and unturned consequences. All humanity does is destroy
the ecosystem. The ecosystem needs to be restored and balanced. The solution that GreenScape offers is to create a habit of
planting trees, plants and flowers. In other words, to bring the green environment back to life by planting them and by learning
how to take care of them. Eventually this will become lifestyle for many of us. Even a single person who plants and who takes care
of the plants counts. One way, Greenscape contributes to creating a habit of planting, is by organizing different events. The theme
of those events is planting different types of trees and plants. All the profit of Greenscape coming from sells will go on providing
sources for planting events across the US. By sharing your experience on your Instagram account and tagging GreenScape
you may become our lucky customer and win free plant of your choice. To spread a word about Greenscape, feel freeto use the hashtag
#READYSETGROW
